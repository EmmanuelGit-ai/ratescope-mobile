# RateScope Business Context

This doc explains WHY RateScope exists. Read this to understand the product vision.

## The Problem: Decision Fatigue

- Americans spend 110 hours/year choosing what to watch (UserTesting/Talker Research 2024, n=4,000)
- Average 25 minutes/week choosing a movie (NOW UK Study, n=2,000)
- 49% of people take so long choosing they give up and watch nothing
- 57 full days across an adult lifetime spent just picking movies
- Netflix users lose interest after 60-90 seconds of browsing
- 61% of consumers feel overwhelmed by choice
- This is "Netflix Syndrome" rooted in the Paradox of Choice (Hick's Law)

RateScope's promise: cut movie selection from 25 minutes to under 2 minutes.

## Competitive Landscape

| Platform | Users | Strengths | Critical Gap |
|----------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| IMDb | 250M+/mo | Largest film database, Amazon ecosystem | No aggregation, no prediction, no decision help |
| Rotten Tomatoes | ~100M/mo | Strong brand, binary fresh/rotten | Oversimplifies nuance, no predictive tools |
| Metacritic | ~25M/mo | Covers games/TV/music, weighted methodology | Small user base, no prediction or discovery |
| Letterboxd | 15M users | Highly engaged niche, social discovery | Niche-only, no prediction, no cross-platform data |
| Cinelytic | B2B only | Warner Bros. client, 19-attribute model | Enterprise-only, not consumer-facing (~$5K/script) |
| Chartmetric | B2B only | Real-time streaming data, artist discovery | Music-only, no consumer rating system |

## The Gap No One Fills

No single platform combines:
1. Multi-source rating aggregation (6+ sources into one five-star score)
2. AI-powered predictive analytics for CONSUMERS (not just studios)
3. A transparent methodology (show the math, not just the number)
4. Decision acceleration tools (mood matching, time-budget, regret prevention)

## 8 Unexplored Niches (Future Feature Pipeline)

1. Regret Prevention Score: predict post-viewing satisfaction using completion rates, rewatch rates, audience sentiment delta (expectation vs reality). Completely novel metric.
2. Rating Momentum Tracking: track real-time rating trajectory post-release. A movie debuting at 8.2 that drops to 6.4 tells a different story than one climbing 7.0 to 8.5.
3. Cross-Cultural Rating Normalization: A 7/10 on Douban (China) differs from 7/10 on IMDb. First culturally-calibrated global rating system.
4. Group Decision Engine: takes multiple taste profiles, finds optimal overlap for co-viewing groups. Completely unserved.
5. Consumer-Facing Predictive Scores: Cinelytic/ScriptBook sell to studios. We package this for everyday users.
6. Time-Aware Recommendations: "90 minutes before bed" yields different results than "Saturday marathon."
7. Unified Movie + Music Rating (Phase 2): First unified entertainment rating system.
8. Regional Cinema Coverage: Nollywood (1,600+ titles/year), Bollywood, Korean cinema. Rating platforms remain Western-centric.

## Why Movies First (Not Music)

Movies won 7/10 metrics in data-driven comparison:

| Metric | Movies | Music | Winner |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Decision Fatigue | 25 min/week | 3-5 min | Movies |
| Prediction Accuracy | 82-90% (rich metadata) | 60-75% (audio-only) | Movies |
| Data Availability | TMDb/OMDb free APIs | No free comprehensive API | Movies |
| Daily Volume | 15-30 new/day | 24,000+ songs/day (800x!) | Movies |
| Time to Build | 16-week MVP | 24+ weeks (audio pipeline) | Movies |
| Revenue Per User | Higher (tickets, streaming) | Lower (ads, subs) | Movies |

Amazon started with books, not "everything." RateScope starts with movies.
Music comes Phase 2 (months 6-10) after movies prove the model.

## Core Value Proposition

- AGGREGATE: Pull ratings from 6+ sources into one normalized five-star score
- PREDICT: AI prediction using 12 modifiers, visible to consumers, not just studios
- DECIDE: Cut 25-minute decisions to under 2 minutes with mood/time-budget tools
- DISCOVER: Genre ratings, score breakdown transparency, mood-based exploration

## Revenue Model

1. Freemium Subscriptions: Free basic; Pro ($4.99/mo) for predictive scores, advanced filters, group tools
2. B2B API Licensing: Predictive analytics API for studios, distributors, streaming platforms
3. Affiliate Partnerships: Deep-link to streaming platforms; earn per click/conversion
4. Advertising: Premium ad placements targeting engaged entertainment consumers
